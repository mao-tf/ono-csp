molecular arrnagement (.xyz file),input files for Gaussian16 calculations and batch job script files are made in make_*.py files.
Molecular optimization with hill-climbing approach is controlled in stepX_*.py files where parameters to be calculated are determined and also the results of DFT calculations are summarized.

Step 1 the optimization of intralayer adjacent 8 molecules without molecular shift along molecular long axis.
Step 2 the molecular shift and torsion is optimized. step2_twist corresponds to the type-3 packing with glide symmetry and step2_para corresponds to type 2 & 4.
Step 3 interlayer molecular arrangement or c-vector is optimized. Optimization workflow for both twist and para are prepared.

utils.py contains utility programs. get_E enable you to obtain calculated intermolecular interaction energy from Gaussian output files. You can rotate molecules with Rod. 

The contents of make and stepX is almost the same other than step2_para. In step2_para energy potential map is calculated with simplfied process where lattice size id fixed to the optimum structure of step1.

Prepare a source directory with python programms and working directory where optimization is held. 
During execution, calcutaed energies are saved in stepX.csv in the working directory.
Also, the program creates a subdirectory named Gaussian and Gaussview.
